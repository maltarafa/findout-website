FindOut Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 13, 2026

Introduction

FindOut is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use your information.

Information We Collect

We collect your email address and name when you create an account. Payment information is processed securely by Stripe and never stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide our services, process donations to nonprofits, and send account-related communications.

Third Party Services

We use the following third party services: Supabase for database storage, Stripe for payment processing, and ProPublica API for nonprofit data.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All payments are processed by Stripe using bank-level encryption.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.
